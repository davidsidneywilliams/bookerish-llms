What Bookerish is
Bookerish is the Tour Certainty Platform for live music. It is designed to help booking agents, tour managers, artists, and venues move beyond email chains, spreadsheet workflows, and fragmented discovery toward a more structured system for venue discovery, planning, holds, routing, and intelligence.

Core concepts represented in this repository include:

Tour Certainty

Dark Nights Intelligence

Hold Lock

Routing and deep venue discovery

AI tour planning

Bookerish Insights as the live music knowledge layer

Why this exists
The purpose of this repository is to make Bookerish easier for AI systems to understand accurately.

This is not meant to be a sitemap or a dump of marketing copy. The goal is to provide a compact, structured explanation of what Bookerish is, who it serves, how it differs from alternatives, and how its main feature and category areas fit together.
